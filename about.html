Journey & Legacy

Life Story of Dr. Madhuram Chowdry

A journey of dedication, perseverance, and an unwavering commitment to healing.

Early Life & Education

The Foundation of Excellence

Dr. Madhuram Chowdry began his medical journey by pursuing MBBS at Mandya Institute of Medical Sciences, Mandya. He chose orthopedics as his specialization to build a stable career early in life after marrying young. Over time, treating fractures and relieving patients from severe pain developed into his true passion. The ability to provide immediate relief and help patients return quickly to normal life motivated his strong dedication to orthopedic practice.

Training & Specialisation

Mastering the Art of Knee Surgery

Dr. Madhuram Chowdry has gained extensive training in orthopedics with a fellowship in sports medicine and joint replacement. He served at Mandya Government Hospital for one year and worked as a visiting consultant in multiple hospitals in Mysore, followed by four years of freelancing practice. He later became Medical Director of A.R. Hospital, building it from scratch, and went on to specialize in knee replacement surgery, with a strong focus on treating chronic knee pain and improving mobility, especially for elderly patients.

Clinical Practice

Building a Legacy of Mobility

Joining A R Hospital, Mysuru, Dr. Chowdry established himself as the city's most trusted knee specialist. With over 10+ years of hands-on experience and 500+ successful knee replacements, he has given hundreds of patients a new lease on life - free from pain and full of movement.

Today & Beyond

A Commitment That Never Stops

Dr. Chowdry continues to push the boundaries of orthopedic care-embracing the latest innovations in robotics, minimally invasive techniques, and rapid recovery protocols. His mission remains simple: help every patient walk pain-free, as quickly and safely as possible.
